Hi, I'm Mikoto — I'm a content creator and IT sales expert based in Amsterdam.

I'm passionate about two things—traveling and technology. As a travel YouTuber, I document my adventures through vlogs, sharing my experiences from various corners of the world. Whether I'm exploring a hidden gem abroad or giving a glimpse into life in Amsterdam, I aim to inspire others to embark on their own journeys. My channel is a blend of destination highlights, travel tips, and authentic day-to-day moments, allowing my audience to experience different cultures and places through my lens. I love connecting with my viewers, providing insights and recommendations that can make their trips unforgettable.

Alongside my love for travel, I work in IT sales and use my expertise to help businesses solve technical problems and grow their digital presence. Whether you're in need of tech solutions, content creation guidance, or a well-planned trip, I'm here to assist!
